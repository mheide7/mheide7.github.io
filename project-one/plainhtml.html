Righteous / Regular

Designed by Brian J. Bonislawsky, Righteous was inspired by the all-capital letterforms in the deco posters of Hungarian artist Robert Berény for Modiano (FontSpring). In Berény’s visionary contradiction, art deco was blended with Hungarian Avant-Garde to create a grid based and geometric machine age design (ArtFixDaily). Utilizing this emphasis of Righteous’ geometric background, this creation focuses on Bonislawsky’s desire to revive old type styles and unearth long lost typefaces with the imagination of a child (Astigmatic).

Madison Heide
