Steady Yourself

Activities

Taking a nap can boost alertness and creativity, as well as give you time to recharge! Meditation can increase blood flow to the brain, as well as increase positive emotions and stability! Calling someone you love is a good idea when you are feeling down because this person cares and can help talk you through whatever is bothering you.

Items

Gratitude books allow for reflection and also help sustain positive emotions for a longer period of time! Weighted blankets create a deep pressure simulation that synthesizes a calming and soothing effect on the body. Eating chocolate creates a melting sensation and the release of endorphins that your body loves!

Resources

Suicide lifeline (Call 800-273-8255 (24/7/365)) (Text 273TALK to 839863 (8am-11pm PST daily)) Call this number if it is ever a concern that you or someone else will harm themselves, remember, your mental health is a priority! Alcohol and Drug Helpline (Call 800-923-4357 (24/7/365)) (Text RecoveryNow to 839863 (8am-11pm PST daily)) Substance abuse is serious and should not be taken lightly, always remember how important taking care of your body is!
