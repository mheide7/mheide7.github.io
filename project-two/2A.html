Chill Time

Activities

Cooking can improve both your mood and your restlessness, as well as enhance mindfulness. Not to mention it provides a tasty treat! Playing with animals can help you lower stress levels and can increase your happiness levels, and allows you to make an awesome new friend! Exercising generates happy chemicals to your brain, and helps release all of the energy being kept up in your body! Volunteering allows you to bring all that energy into something beneficial: helping others!

Items

Board Games lower blood pressure and help relax you. Not only will board games allow you to focus, but they will help you strengthen relationships with those you are playing with! Car Rides (going for a drive) sometimes just getting out of the room helps make you adjust your mood. The scenery can help relax and boost your mood as well! Painting allows you to fully commit and engage yourself into an activity. Not only is it fun, it reduces stress!

Resources

Calm (app) gives the user a multitude of activities to engage with, the personalization of the app is another huge benefit! Counseling Services or Online Services it is okay to ask for help, and seeking out various counseling services or websites provide a good opportunity for that initial contact.
